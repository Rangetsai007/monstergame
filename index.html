About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed roguelike game where your objective is simple: survive as long as possible against waves of increasingly difficult monsters. As you progress, you'll unlock new abilities, weapons, and power-ups to enhance your character.

The game features procedurally generated levels, ensuring that no two runs are ever the same. With its addictive gameplay loop and strategic depth, Monster Survivors offers hours of challenging entertainment for gamers of all skill levels.

Inspired by popular roguelike titles, Monster Survivors combines fast-paced action with strategic decision-making to create a truly engaging gaming experience.

How to Play

Controls

  • Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character
  • Press Space to activate special abilities
  • Collect experience orbs to level up
  • Choose new abilities or upgrades when leveling up

Tips for Success

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded
  • Prioritize defensive upgrades early game
  • Focus on building a synergistic set of abilities
  • Don't be afraid to restart - each run makes you better!
